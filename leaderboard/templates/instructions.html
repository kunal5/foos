Instructions to run



  1. Open Django Admin with given credentials.
  2. Select Player->Add Player and add all players names
  3. Go back and choose Team->Add Team and teams with team member details and team name.
  4. Go back and choose Game->Add Game to register all games between respective teams in the tournament.
  5. Go back and add Scores of all teams for a game.
  6. Go back and enter the winner of game for that game.
  7. After all information is saved, go back and see GAME STATS, TEAM STATS, LEADERBOARD.
